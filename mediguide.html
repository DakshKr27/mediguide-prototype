Drug Interaction Checker

Enter medicine names to detect dangerous combinations, overdose risks & safety alerts.

Interaction Results
🚨
Aspirin + Ibuprofen — DANGER
Both are NSAIDs. Taking together significantly increases risk of gastrointestinal bleeding and reduces the cardioprotective effect of Aspirin. Avoid combination. Consult your doctor immediately.
✅
Aspirin + Metformin — SAFE
No significant interaction detected. Aspirin may slightly enhance the glucose-lowering effect of Metformin in some patients — monitor blood sugar levels.
✅
Pantoprazole + Metformin — SAFE
No clinically significant interaction. Pantoprazole may reduce absorption of Metformin slightly. Take Metformin with meals to minimize this effect.
⚠️
Aspirin + Pantoprazole — CAUTION
Pantoprazole is often prescribed alongside Aspirin to protect the stomach lining — this is intentional. However, avoid taking them at the exact same time. Take Pantoprazole 30 min before Aspirin.
